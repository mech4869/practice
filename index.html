4 Wheel Kitcken

4 Wheel Kitchen is a food truck with a full kitchen on board to offer our customers the freshest food prepared as they place their order. No packaged or premade stuff served to our fans who want to eat healthy.

Our team on the truck starts each day at the base kitchen, gathering the day’s ingredients, organizing menu items, loading the truck and heading out for the day’s destination on the street. Fridays and Saturdays we work both a lunch and dinner schedule.
